What is HP-NewsCraft?

HP-NewsCraft is a simple image-based role-playing bulletin board where everyone pretends the world is ending and shares fake post apocalyptic news. Everyone can comment and discuss how they are going to survive or how their life is going right now in the fictional world. Feel free to share your artistic works of fiction.
